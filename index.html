What is SMSTaxi?

SMSTaxi ultalizes widespread cellular technology to bring the ridesharing to the masses. Current ridesharing platforms provide convient, fast, and affordable transportation to many. Unfortunatly, current systems require all users to use smartphones and have an internet connection. This excludes two large groups of people: those without smartphones and those without data. These two groups represent almost 25% of the American population and many more overseas. In India, only 22% of the population own a smartphone, creating a large market for SMSTaxi. Further, SMSTaxi will soon be cross-platform, providing users with more ride options.

How does SMSTaxi work?

SMSTaxi was built on top of some of the best technology available. The messaging service and centralized number is powered by Twillo. The Lyft API is used to search for drivers and keep payments secure. This entire system is hosted on Microsoft's Azure server, an essential component for connecting the other technologies. The platform is written primarily in Node.js with a few other languages sprinkled in.
